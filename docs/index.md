## What this app does

Hanzi Stroke Practice helps you **generate printable Chinese handwriting practice sheets** with correct **stroke order** on your iPhone or iPad.

- Type or paste Chinese text; the app builds a practice sheet on **your device** — no account required.
- Each character shows stroke sequence and guidelines for tracing practice.
- **Preview** the sheet in the app, then **save**, **share**, or **print** as PDF through standard iOS system dialogs.
- **Local-first:** your input is not sent to our servers (we do not operate backend services for this app).
- **No ads** and **no cross-app tracking** in the current version.
- Language UI: follow system, English, or 简体中文 (About → Language).

---

## Character dictionary coverage

Stroke data comes from a **bundled dictionary** (`hanzi_dictionary.json`) derived from the open [Make Me a Hanzi](https://github.com/skishore/makemeahanzi) project.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Approximate coverage** | **~9,000+** simplified Chinese characters (常用汉字范围) with stroke-order outlines |
| **Processing** | Entirely on-device lookup and rendering |
| **Not in dictionary** | Characters outside the bundled set may not show full stroke animation; the app will indicate when data is unavailable |

If you need a character that is missing, try a common variant or contact us — we may expand the dictionary in future updates.
