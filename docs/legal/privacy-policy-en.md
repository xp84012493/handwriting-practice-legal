## Summary

Hanzi Stroke Practice is a **local-first** app for generating Chinese stroke-order practice sheets. We **do not require an account**, **do not sell your data**, and **do not use advertising or cross-app tracking** in the current version.

---

## Information we collect

### Data you provide

- **Chinese characters you type** to generate practice sheets. This text is processed **on your device** to look up stroke data and render the sheet. We do **not** transmit your input to our servers (we do not operate backend servers for this app).

### Data stored on your device

- **Language preference** (follow system / Chinese / English), saved with `shared_preferences` on device only.
- **Generated PDFs** when you use Save or Share — handled by the **system** (Files, Share Sheet, Print). We do not receive copies.

### Data we do not collect

- Name, email, phone number, or Apple ID (unless you email us voluntarily).
- Precise location.
- Contacts, photos, or microphone/camera content (the app does not request these permissions in the current version).
- Analytics, advertising identifiers, or crash reports **unless you add such SDKs in a future version** — if we add them, we will update this policy and App Store privacy labels.

---

## How we use information

On-device processing is used only to:

- Load the bundled stroke dictionary (`hanzi_dictionary.json`, derived from [Make Me a Hanzi](https://github.com/skishore/makemeahanzi)).
- Render previews and export PDFs.
- Remember your language setting.

---

## Third-party services

- **Apple App Store / iOS:** distribution, updates, and system print/share APIs.
- **Stroke outline data:** Make Me a Hanzi / Arphic Public License — see in-app **About → Third-party notices**.

The app does **not** embed third-party analytics or ad networks in the current version.

---

## Children

The app is suitable for general audiences learning Chinese writing. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us by email, contact [SUPPORT_EMAIL].

## Your choices

- You can delete the app to remove on-device preferences.
- You can change language in **About → Language**.
- You can avoid using Print/Share if you do not want to save PDFs outside the app.

---

## International users

The app is available in multiple regions. Data described above stays on your device except when you choose to share or print through Apple’s system dialogs.
